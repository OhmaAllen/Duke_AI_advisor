Xiaowei Yang is a Professor of Computer Science at Duke University, specializing in networking, systems, and security. She currently holds multiple appointments, including Co-Director of Graduate Studies for the Master of Science in Economics and Computation Program and Director of Graduate Studies for the MS Program in the Department of Computer Science. Yang earned her Ph.D. from MIT in 2004 and her B.S.E. from Tsinghua University in 1996.: xiaoweiyang
