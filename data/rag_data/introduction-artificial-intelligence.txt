COMPSCI 370D: introduction-artificial-intelligence
Algorithms and representations used in artificial intelligence. Introduction and implementation of algorithms for search, planning, decision, theory, logic, Bayesian networks, robotics and machine learning. Prerequisite: Computer Science 201 and one of the following: Computer Science 230, 200-level Mathematics course, or 200-level Statistical Science course.
